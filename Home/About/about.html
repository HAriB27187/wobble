About Us

Learn about Wobble Lab's journey, purpose, and team

Company Overview

Founded in 2018, Wobble Lab is a leading force in technology innovation—specializing in drone technology, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things (IoT). Our core mission is to empower India's youth to become next-generation tech leaders by equipping them with the latest industry-relevant knowledge and hands-on experience.

What began as a small initiative has evolved into a fast-growing company known for its excellence and impactful solutions. From drone training programs to developing customized tech systems for industries, Wobble Lab continues to push boundaries and redefine innovation.

Our strength lies in our team—experienced trainers and technical experts who are dedicated to the success of every student and client we work with. We believe in building a tech-driven future for India that competes confidently on the global stage.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To empower youth through the latest technologies and develop them as tech entrepreneurs and innovative thinkers. Our goal is to contribute to strengthening India's tech ecosystem and prepare the next generation of innovators.

Our Vision

To become the first preferred center for technical education and innovation in India. We want every student and client who connects with us to establish their identity in the technological world and actively contribute to the country's technological development.
